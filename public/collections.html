Eid Al Adha - Morocco

Preparing ram for slaughter

The patriarch of the family prepares for ritual slaughter of ram. Moroccans typically wait until the King slaughters his ram to commence their own Eid celebrations. Private residence in Dwar Haja, Rabat.

Street Life and Markets - Morocco

Muhammed 6

Portraits of Morocco's King Muhammed 6, or M 6, are common in public places. Fielty to the crown is a major part of national identity. Criticism of the king himself is not tolerated.

Moroccan Independence Day Celebration

Waving flags and portraits of the King, revelers gather in central Rabat to mark Moroccan Independence Day.
